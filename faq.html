FAQ

FAQ

(FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS)

Q:
Why should I choose Kenme?
A:

Kenme is one of the leading and first- standard online language training institute that offers the best training to excel in English language.

Q:
What is the duration of the course?
A:

Duration is depended upon your course. For the Entry and Secondary level courses, the duration will be 60 days and for advanced level, it is for 30 days.

Q:
When can I attend the classes? Is there a fixed schedule?
A:

There is no fixed time to attend the classes. As the personal tutor is available from 9-11, you can attend the lessons according to your convenience and submit the works to your assigned personal tutor.

Q:
Will I get a personal tutor till the end of my course?
A:

Of course, personal tutors are assigned to each student and will be helping you till the end of your classes. They will check your home works and give feedbacks along with keeping you motivated and enthusiastic.

Q:
What are the qualifications of the trainers?
A:

All our trainers are well qualified with post- graduation in English and IELTS qualified and are having years of experience in teaching.

Q:
I am very confused in grammar. Do you provide grammar lessons?
A:

Certainly. Our grammar lessons are tailor- made for the students to grasp the ideas quickly and understand the topics in an easy way.

Q:
Can I visit your office?
A:

You are always welcome. We are located at Mini bypass road puthiyara Calicut, Calicut.

Q:
How can I improve my communication if I join the classes?
A:

We are assigning personal tutors; you can interact with them through speaking sessions. Along with that, you will be given a calling activity tutor, with them you can converse through calls and improve communication skills.

Q:
Will you provide learning materials throughout the class?
A:

Yes, we do. You will be provided with learning materials on all the topics that are being taught here. All the learning aids are easy and precise for easy comprehension.
